What We Offer

ATANI
SERVICES

From fiber optic backbones to RF broadcast networks, Atani Satellite Services engineers end-to-end transmission solutions for businesses and operators who demand performance, flexibility and control.

01

Business Network Solutions

  • Dedicated Corporate Networks
  • LAN Interconnect Services
  • Managed IP-Based VPN Services
At Atani Satellite Services Limited, we design and deliver corporate network solutions that seamlessly connect central and remote offices while enabling access to global networks. Our networks provide high-speed internet connectivity alongside advanced data management and transmission capabilities. Each solution is built with scalability in mind, allowing for future expansion, additional services, and increased usage. We also integrate separate LANs into a unified wide area network, ensuring efficient, reliable communication across your organization.
02

Fiber Optic Solutions

  • Route Survey, Cable Laying, Splicing & Termination
  • Testing & Commissioning
  • As-Built Diagram
At Atani Satellite Services Limited, we leverage the extensive training and experience of our personnel to deliver robust fiber optic network designs. Our project teams are equipped with state-of-the-art tools and testing equipment to ensure fast, reliable deployments. We apply proven project management practices to monitor progress and achieve timely delivery. We also have strong experience in managing community-related issues during fiber optic deployment projects.
03

RF Network & Broadcast Solutions

  • BTS Backhauls
  • Large Earth Station & VSAT Installations
  • Broadband Setup and Installation
  • RF Verification, Equipment Testing & Commissioning
At Atani Satellite Services Limited, we provide RF network planning, design, and implementation services, including link budget analysis and calculations for satellite and microwave links. We install and maintain large satellite earth stations, VSAT BTS backhaul infrastructure, and microwave transmission systems, as well as broadcast infrastructure. As a systems integration company, our customers benefit from our extensive experience and technical expertise.
04

Professional Services

  • Training & Support Services
  • Consultancy Services
  • Project Management Services
At Atani Satellite Services Limited, we deliver customized training programs to build capacity across all levels, from field engineers to senior management. We provide advanced consulting services to support network rollout and optimize existing infrastructure, with a strong focus on transmission performance. We also offer comprehensive system integration solutions for telecommunications operators and carriers, alongside technical network audits to ensure efficient utilization of infrastructure.

Who we are

ABOUT
ATANI

At Atani Satellite Services Limited, we recognize that our greatest strength is our people. We are therefore committed to investing in targeted training and professional development to ensure effective, high-quality service delivery to our customers. Our team has undertaken training in VSAT Technology and applications, CASS-B-MAX system specialist, TV and Radio broadcast installations, Fiber optic planning, design and deployment.

Our Mission

Our mission is to design, build, and deliver high-speed transmission infrastructure that meets the specific needs of businesses and network operators. We strive to understand each customer's unique requirements, develop cost-effective solutions, and provide reliable platforms that seamlessly integrate voice, data, and multimedia services using advanced technology.

Our Vision

Our vision is to empower customers with greater choice, flexibility, and control over their voice and data networks by continuously delivering innovative, scalable, and customer-focused connectivity solutions.
